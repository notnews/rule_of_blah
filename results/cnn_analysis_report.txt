KEY FINDINGS
======================================================================

1. Nearly half (49.7%) of CNN articles covering court decisions
   mention who appointed the judges involved.

2. Implicit mentions (54.5%) outnumber explicit mentions (40.0%),
   showing the importance of LLM-based detection over regex.

3. Trump appointees are mentioned most frequently (42.7% of all
   president mentions), reflecting coverage of the conservative
   Supreme Court majority he established.

4. Quote verification rate is high (~87%), indicating the LLM
   is providing grounded evidence from the articles.
